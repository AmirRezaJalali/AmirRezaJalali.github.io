About me

I am an antenna designer engineer. I design Multiband and Ultra-WideBand antennas. I graduated from University of Sistan and Baluchestan with a Masters degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering. I worked under supervision of Prof. Javad Ahmadi-Shokouh. For my master project, I worked on designing different multiband antennas, Moreover, I’ve recently joined a group in School of Electrical and Computer Engineering whose main purpose is designing ultra-wide band antenna for ultra-wide band systems.

My major areas of interest are:
  • Designing multiband antennas
  • Designing ultra-wide band antennas
